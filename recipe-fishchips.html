Fish and Chips

Fish and chips is a famous British dish known for its crispy fried fish and golden fries. This simple recipe delivers a crunchy texture and delicious flavor that makes it perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • Fish fillets
  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Oil

Instructions

Coat fish in batter and fry until golden. Cut potatoes into fries and fry separately. Serve hot with salt and sauce.
