Classic Beef Burger

The classic beef burger is a timeless favorite enjoyed around the world. With a juicy grilled patty, fresh vegetables, and soft buns, this burger delivers incredible flavor in every bite. This simple recipe helps you create a restaurant-quality burger at home using fresh ingredients and easy cooking techniques.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese (optional)

Instructions

Shape the beef into patties and season with salt and pepper. Grill or cook in a pan until done to your preference. Toast the buns lightly, then assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, and the cooked patty. Add cheese or sauce if desired and serve immediately while hot.
